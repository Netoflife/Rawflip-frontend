About Us

Learn about RawFlip — Nigeria's trusted peer-to-peer digital marketplace.

🌍 Who We Are

RawFlip is a Nigerian peer-to-peer digital marketplace that enables users to buy, sell, and trade items securely using an internal wallet system.

Our platform is designed to create a structured and transparent trading environment where users can transact with confidence. By combining wallet-based payments, transaction state controls, and administrative oversight, RawFlip reduces fraud risks and improves trust between buyers and sellers.

🎯 Our Mission

Our mission is to build a secure and efficient digital marketplace tailored to the Nigerian ecosystem, where users can exchange value safely and reliably.

📦 What We Offer

  • Secure wallet-based deposits and withdrawals
  • Structured transaction lifecycle system
  • Transparent fee structure
  • Fraud prevention mechanisms
  • Administrative review for financial transactions

🤝 Our Commitment

We are committed to:

  • Protecting user data
  • Ensuring financial accuracy
  • Maintaining platform integrity
  • Continuously improving security standards

Help & Support

Get answers fast or reach our team directly. We usually respond within 24 hours.

💳 Wallet & Payments

How do I add money to my wallet? ▾
Go to Wallet → Deposit. Enter the amount and transfer the exact sum to our displayed bank account. Once we confirm your payment (usually within 2 hours during business hours), your balance will be credited. Always use your displayed payment reference.
How long does a withdrawal take? ▾
Withdrawals are processed within 1–2 business days. You will receive an email confirmation once approved. Ensure your bank details in your profile are correct before requesting.
What are the transaction fees? ▾
Free plan: 5% platform fee per sale. Basic plan: 3%. Pro plan: 1.5%. Early adopter members (first 200 users): 0% for 6 months. Fees are deducted automatically when a transaction completes.
My deposit isn't showing. What do I do? ▾
First, double-check that you transferred the exact amount and included the payment reference. If it's been more than 4 business hours, open a support ticket below with your bank transfer receipt attached.

🔒 Escrow & Transactions

How does escrow work? ▾
When you accept an offer, the buyer's funds are held securely in escrow. The seller ships the item and marks it dispatched. The buyer confirms receipt, releasing the funds to the seller. This protects both parties.
What happens if the seller doesn't ship? ▾
If the seller doesn't mark an item as shipped within the agreed timeframe, you can open a dispute. Our team will review and issue a full refund to your wallet if the seller cannot provide proof of dispatch.
How long until funds auto-release to the seller? ▾
Once the seller marks an item as shipped, the buyer has 5 days to confirm receipt or raise a dispute. After 5 days with no action, funds auto-release to the seller. You'll receive a 24-hour warning email before auto-release.
Can I cancel a transaction? ▾
Transactions can be cancelled by mutual agreement before shipping. Once an item is marked as shipped, you'll need to open a dispute if there's a problem. Contact the other party through messages first.

⚠️ Disputes & Refunds

How do I open a dispute? ▾
Go to Transactions → [your transaction] → Open Dispute. Describe the issue clearly and attach evidence (photos, screenshots). Our team aims to resolve disputes within 3–5 business days.
What evidence should I provide in a dispute? ▾
For buyers: photos of item received vs listing photos, screenshots of seller messages, any delivery receipts. For sellers: proof of dispatch (courier receipt, tracking number), photos of item before shipping.
How are refunds processed? ▾
Approved refunds go back to your RawFlip wallet balance immediately. From there you can withdraw to your bank account. We do not issue refunds directly to bank accounts — they always go to your wallet first.

👤 Account & Listings

How do I get a verified seller badge? ▾
Verified badges are awarded to Pro plan subscribers and Early Adopters (first 200 users). Complete your profile, maintain a positive review score, and stay active to be considered.
How many listings can I have? ▾
Free plan: up to 5 active listings. Basic plan: up to 20. Pro plan: unlimited. Early adopters enjoy unlimited listings free for 6 months.
My account was suspended. What now? ▾
Suspensions happen for violations of our Terms of Service. Open a support ticket below to appeal. Include your username and the reason you believe the suspension was made in error. We review all appeals.
How do I delete my account? ▾
Go to Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Account. Note: you cannot delete your account while you have pending transactions or an outstanding wallet balance. Withdraw or complete all transactions first.
